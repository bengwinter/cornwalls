Cornwall's

Cornwall's is a drinking and dining establishment that is, first and foremost, a British-style pub, where people congregate to drink their pints of beer and ale, talk, play games and eat. Cornwall's caters to those who want a good meal and a good drink at regular prices. The food and drink are traditional in a good, solid way. Cornwall's was started on the premise that drinking makes you hungry and eating makes you thirsty, and both sets of circumstances, while accompanied by good conversation and a relaxed atmosphere, combine to create a resilient institution - which contributes to community life.
